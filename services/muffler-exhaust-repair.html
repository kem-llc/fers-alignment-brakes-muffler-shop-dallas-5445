Muffler & Exhaust Repair

Expert exhaust system repair from minor leaks to full replacements.

Schedule this service

Overview

A damaged exhaust system can reduce fuel efficiency, create loud noise, and allow dangerous fumes into the cabin. We repair or replace mufflers, catalytic converters, oxygen sensors, and exhaust pipes. We work with all makes and models.

What's included

  • Reduces engine noise and vibration
  • Improves fuel economy and engine performance
  • Ensures emissions compliance
  • Warranty on all exhaust parts

How it works

1

Diagnosis

We inspect the entire exhaust system for leaks, rust, and damage using visual and pressure tests.

2

Repair/Replace

We replace damaged sections with quality aftermarket or OEM parts and weld or clamp connections securely.

3

Final Check

We start the engine, check for leaks, and ensure proper exhaust flow and noise levels.

Common questions

Is a loud exhaust always a problem?

Not always, but it often indicates a leak or failing muffler. We can inspect and advise.

Do you replace catalytic converters?

Yes, we replace catalytic converters and can help with emissions-related check engine lights.
