Wheel Alignment

Extend tire life and improve handling with precision computerized alignment.

Overview

Proper wheel alignment is crucial for safe driving and tire longevity. Our advanced alignment system measures angles to manufacturer specifications, correcting camber, caster, and toe. We service all passenger cars, SUVs, and light trucks.

What's included

  • Reduces uneven tire wear
  • Improves fuel economy
  • Enhances steering and handling
  • Prevents vibration and pulling

How it works

1

Inspection

We check tire condition, suspension components, and current alignment angles.

2

Adjustment

Using computerized equipment, we adjust alignment to your vehicle’s exact specifications.

3

Test Drive

We take your car for a road test to ensure everything feels right.

Common questions

How often should I get an alignment?

Most manufacturers recommend every 6,000 miles or if you notice pulling, uneven tire wear, or after hitting a large pothole.

How long does an alignment take?

Typically 30 to 60 minutes depending on your vehicle.
