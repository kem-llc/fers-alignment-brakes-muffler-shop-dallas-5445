Our story

Your Neighborhood Auto Shop Since 1995

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair at fair prices, earning the trust of Dallas drivers one vehicle at a time.

Fer’s Alignment & Brakes opened its doors in 1995 with a simple mission: deliver expert auto repair with integrity. What started as a two-bay shop on Carroll Avenue has grown into a trusted destination for drivers across Dallas. Our founder, a master technician with over 30 years of experience, built the business on the belief that every customer deserves transparent communication and skilled workmanship.

Today, we continue that legacy. Our team of ASE-certified mechanics uses state-of-the-art equipment to service alignments, brakes, exhaust systems, and more. We treat every car as if it belongs to a family member, and we back our repairs with a solid warranty. Whether you need a quick alignment or a full brake job, Fer’s is here to keep you safe on the road.
