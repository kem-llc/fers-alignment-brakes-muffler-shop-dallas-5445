Our story

Family-Owned Auto Care in Dallas Since 1995

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair at fair prices, treating every customer like a neighbor and every car like our own.

Fer’s Alignment & Brakes was founded in 1995 by Fernando “Fer” Martinez, a master mechanic with a passion for precision and a commitment to integrity. Starting as a small two-bay shop on Carroll Avenue, Fer built a loyal following by doing the job right the first time and never upselling unnecessary work. Over the years, the shop grew as word spread about Fer’s honest pricing and expert craftsmanship.

Today, Fer’s is still family-owned and operated, serving drivers from Lakewood to Deep Ellum and beyond. Our team of ASE-certified technicians continues Fer’s legacy of transparency, quality, and community. Whether it’s a simple alignment or a complex brake job, we treat every vehicle with the same care we’d give our own.
